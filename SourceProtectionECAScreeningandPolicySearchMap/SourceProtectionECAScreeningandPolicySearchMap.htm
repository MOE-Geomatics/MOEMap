Source Protection ECA Screening and Policy Search Map

This tool enables a user to identify whether an address falls within a Vulnerable Area. The tool then allows the user to identify significant threat Source Protection Policies for that zone.

Note: Zones where Significant threats appear are Wellhead Protection Areas:WHPA A; and WHPA B with scores of 10 or 8; Intake Protection Zones - IPZ 1 and 2 with scores greater than 7, and Issue Contributing Areas. For the threat activityDense Non Aqueous Phase Liquids (DNAPL) the significant threat zone is broader, encompassing WHPA C. Threat Activities and the Circumstances under which they pose a threat to drinking water are searchable in the link below.
